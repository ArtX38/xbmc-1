What is it?

Essence is a skin for the cross-platform media center solution name XBMC. The goal of Essence is to provide a simple and high degree of customization's in an elegant skin.

  • Made for 1080p.
  • "Live-Preivew" of how the different skin options affect the skin.
  • Most features are options that can be disabled/enabled by the user for those with low-end hardware.
  • Different sets of styles available, with a beautiful dark/glass as default. More sets to be available for download.
